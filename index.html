I am Timor, a photographer captivated by the raw beauty of our world. Whether it's the towering peaks of Patagonia or the quiet, serene landscapes of Switzerland, my lens is always focused on storytelling through nature and culture. As I prepare for my upcoming move to Bern, I find myself looking for those fleeting, authentic moments that define a place. My work is not just about documenting what I see; it's about translating the feeling of independence, the spirit of travel, and the unique textures of the cultures I encounter into visual narratives. Welcome to my journey.
